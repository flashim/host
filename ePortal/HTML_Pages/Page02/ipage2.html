Tailor the course

Once you've determined that tailoring is necessary, several additional considerations remain.

KPMG Learning courses have been designed to include enough time to allow instructors to include client specific examples and explanations. As long as you make minor adjustments to the content or add/remove minimal amounts of material, the course remains a KPMG Learning course and does not require additional review. If a significant course revision is required, the following firm policies apply:

  • Courses should be reviewed by the engagement partner and if the partner deems necessary, by an additional field subject matter professional with considerable knowledge and experience in the specific content area.
  • Courses revised to the extent that the field of study is impacted or the length of the program increases or decreases more than 15% may have Continuing Professional Education (CPE) implications (refer to the CPE Toolkit section for more information).
