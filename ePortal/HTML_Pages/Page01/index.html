Using KPMG Learning

KPMG Learning offers easy access to a multitude of training courses that cover prime areas of interest, including changing standards covering revenue recognition, fair value and leases. Via the internal portal, KPMG partners and professionals can search for and identify training courses that they share with their clients in the marketplace. These training modules are available in an array of learning modalities such as traditional classroom, live on-line sessions (via the KPMG Institutes) and web-based self-study.

But KPMG Learning is more than just a valuable training resource. It’s a "door opener" to your clients and targets. KPMG Learning gives you a terrific platform from which you can present your strategies and ideas to clients and prospects to address current challenges or future opportunities. Studies have clearly demonstrated that training and education are excellent ways to enhance business relationships. Equally important, KPMG Learning can help your clients fill “skill gaps” in specific topics or issues.

To further enhance your relationship with clients and targets, KPMG has created an external version of KPMG Learning. It offers the same training, resources and in-depth technical knowledge found in the internal version directly to the marketplace. KPMG has been providing executive training and education to clients for more than 20 years, and our programs are well known and highly regarded throughout the industry.

With KPMG Learning, clients have direct access to risk-approved and easy-to-use web-based training modules and study materials covering today’s key issues (refer to the Course Catalog for a complete list). Users can also search for and find upcoming webcasts, Executive Education public offerings, and identify certain programs that can be taught on site at the client/target location.

Massive changes are underway across the accounting industry, and most of these changes have significantly accounting and reporting implications. Standards and best practices are also evolving rapidly. Now more than ever, our clients and targets need valuable and timely help "cutting through complexity"—to effectively manage their business and remain profitable.

KPMG Learning is the latest example of how KPMG is delivering on its corporate mission. The internal portal and the external Web site provide valuable thought leadership and demonstrate your credibility in understanding accounting issues, which will enhance your business relationships.
