Independence

What are the KPMG “Independence” requirements with regards to delivering training services to my client?

One of the most important aspects of our “promise of professionalism” is the requirement to be independent in fact and perception. Compliance with independence standards is a professional responsibility prerequisite to providing audit, review and other attestation services. This pledge to independence is essential to serving the best interests of our clients and their investors -- and to furthering KPMG’s reputation for integrity and quality.

In certain instances, delivery of training to our audit clients (public and non-public) may raise independence questions. Please refer to the Independence Matrix to determine whether your particular client situation is subject to Audit Committee pre-approval, Sentinel, or CLEAS.
