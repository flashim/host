Frequently Asked Questions

Course topics

Who developed the training modules?
The modules were developed by KPMG professionals with the oversight and direction of thought leaders from the KPMG Accounting Group within the Department of Professional Practice (DPP), who regularly consult with key stakeholders on a broad array of today’s crucial technical accounting topics across many industries.
What topic areas does KPMG Learning cover?
The courses cover numerous, critical topics, such as IFRS, FASB updates, SEC reporting and compliance, acquisitions and buyouts, accounting for income tax, and many others. Please consult Course Catalog for a complete listing.
What if my client needs a customized course – can this be done?
Yes – materials available through KPMG Learning can be customized for a particular client. This customization should only be undertaken by subject matter professionals well-grounded in the specific topic area. It should be noted that significant customization is subject to the firm’s QA process.

If your client is looking for a fully customized course, the KPMG Learning team can provide you with guidance on developing training for delivery to clients. Please contact the KPMG Learning Management Team for more information.
How do I get access to courses that are delivered internally but not on the portal?
Many of the courses that are delivered internally are available through the KPMG Learning portal. In the event you are seeking a course or topic that is not yet available through KPMG Learning, the KPMG Learning Management Team can research the course availability for you.
Are non-technical accounting courses available?
At the current time, the KPMG Learning catalog consists primarily of technical accounting courses. As the catalog expands, we envision adding courses on Dodd-Frank, Sarbanes Oxley, Cloud Computing and similar topics.
Can my client or target access KPMG Executive Education courses through KPMG Learning?
Yes, clients and targets interested in attending a KPMG Executive Education course may register for any Executive Education course through KPMG Learning. In addition, registration will also be completed via the established KPMG Executive Education website.
