General topics

What is KPMG Learning?

KPMG Learning is KPMG’s answer to what our professionals, our clients and the marketplace have asked for: a one-stop shop for quality training in technical accounting and related topics in formats geared to various learning modes –web-based self-study, podcasts, webcasts, webinars and classroom seminars.

KPMG Learning hosts a catalog of KPMG training courses that can be accessed in two ways:

  • KPMG Learning internal portal: Here, KPMG Partners and employees can search for and download risk-reviewed courses and other instructor-led training materials that can then be delivered to clients. The programs found on KPMG Learning can be recommended to clients to help them close skill gaps, to accumulate CPE credits, or for general developmental purposes.
  • KPMG Learning external website is accessible to the general public, so that clients, targets and the public at large can directly access KPMG Learning training materials, if they so choose.
What type of content can I find on KPMG Learning?
Over 150 training courses – with many more to come. These courses come from existing firm sources including KPMG’s internal award-winning training curriculum, KPMG Institutes Webcasts and KPMG’s highly rated Executive Education curriculum of group live events. Complementing these well-established learning modes is a new offering – web-based training (WBT) self-study courses that allow users to access and complete self-study training modules online for CPE credit.
How can I use KPMG Learning?
KPMG partners and professionals can use the KPMG Learning catalog to identify training courses that might be of interest to their clients or targets. From the internal portal, engagement teams can download “classroom ready” course that they can deliver directly to their clients. The catalog is searchable by topic, level, mode and # of CPE. The internal portal also offers information on how to qualify courses for CPE, quality assurance requirements and independence guidelines. The portal also contains guidance on how to ensure the courses you deliver are impactful and bring greater value to our clients. Finally, through a link to the KPMG Learning external web site, you can become familiar with the online tool our clients and targets can access directly.
Can I use KPMG Learning for my own training needs?
No. KPMG partners and professionals should use the Focus system for any training.
Do clients have direct access to KPMG Learning courses?
The KPMG Learning external website makes it easy for clients, targets and other external partners easier to access and utilize our training materials.
What is the relationship between KPMG Learning and KPMG Executive Education?
KPMG's Executive Education has been providing continuing education programs for corporate executives and their staffs in the accounting, finance and business management professions for over 20 years. KPMG Executive Education will continue to operate as a provider of training but will do so as an entity within the overall KPMG Learning charter.
