Quality Assurance

Have the courses on KPMG Learning been pre-approved?

Yes. All courses in the KPMG Learning catalog have been reviewed for currency and technical accuracy, and are approved for use with both clients and targets. No additional content approvals are required if the courses are delivered as-designed.

If I need to change or alter a course content, what approvals are needed before I deliver the course to a client?

We are aware that, in some cases, as-designed training content may not meet the specific needs of your client or target, and tailoring or revision is required. Knowing how to obtain the appropriate internal approvals prior to providing training externally is vital to our organization.

According to firm policy, any significant revisions should be reviewed by the engagement partner and if the partner deems necessary, by an additional field subject matter professional with considerable knowledge and experience in the specific content area.

In addition, revising a course to the extent that the field of study is impacted or the length of the program increases or decreases more than 15% may have Continuing Professional Education (CPE) implications (refer to the CPE Toolkit section for more information).

Who will need to review my training content during the QA process?

Significant revisions should be reviewed by the engagement partner and if the partner deems necessary, by an additional field subject matter professional with considerable knowledge and experience in the specific content area.

Do I still need to go through a discrete B&RC review process? How can I ensure that my content is consistent with B&RC policy?
The B&RC review step has been eliminated for most content, as key support groups, such as NDPPS Editorial, have been trained on B&RC policies.
When should I go through NDPPS Editorial?
NDPPS Editorial review should be consulted BEFORE submission to the engagement partner for review.
