Once the training session is completed, there are a number of actions you can take to ensure overall success and specific requirements must be met if the attendees will be earning CPE for participation. They include:

  • Ask participants to complete evaluation forms and provide specific feedback on the training. (CPE Requirement)
  • Compile an overview or summary of the training session for future reference.
  • Participants sign a CPE sign-in sheet. (CPE Requirement)
  • Hand out and collect exams (if used) and process according to exam design plan.Collect all leftover materials; keep or destroy in accordance with the firm's retention guidelines.
  • Follow up with participants to verify their CPE credits were awarded and to learn how they applied or used the course materials.
  • Follow up with the client to see if you can provide any further assistance.

Special Notice: If you created a new course, consider send the course materials to the KPMG Learning Management Team. New courses will be assessed and included in the KPMG Learning course catalog as appropriate.
