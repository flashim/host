Training Delivery Guidance

On the surface, training development and delivery seems simple. Course materials are created, the venue is selected, and the training is conducted. Yet, what separates a great course that fulfills its objectives from a weak course that misses its mark and puts people to sleep?

Here's what our KPMG KPMG Learning training specialists have learned through experience: when KPMG practitioners and their clients have agreed on a course’s purpose, structure, and content, then the training is impactful and KPMG brings greater value to our clients.

Great training programs don't come together by accident. It’s a complete and thorough process – from obtaining a solid understanding of your client’s specific needs, to excellent instruction, to finalizing the course in an accurate and timely way. And there are many smaller but significant steps in between. It all contributes to providing your client with the superior value they expect from an organization like KPMG.
