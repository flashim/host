CPE topics

Can all the KPMG Learning courses be used for CPE credit?
Yes. All courses on KPMG Learning fully qualify for CPE credit, and the modules meet all standards of the National Association of State Boards of Accountancy (NASBA). KPMG is an accredited organization of NASBA as well as a sponsor of CPE credit qualified continuing professional education on the National Registry of CPE Sponsors. The KPMG Learning web-based self study programs are designed so that “Certificates of Completion” can be delivered immediately upon completion of the course.
What is the “CPE Toolkit” and how can I use it?
The CPE Toolkit provides guidance and forms needed to submit an externally delivered course for CPE credit. Following the CPE toolkit requirements prior, during, and after delivering training helps ensure your clients will receive the CPE credits for the courses you provide. More information is available in the CPE Toolkit section of KPMG Learning.
What is QAS Sponsorship?
Quality Assurance Service (QAS) is a set of standards for Web-based self-study programs, defined by the National Association of State Boards of Accountancy (NASBA). Certain states (listed below) will only accept CPE credit for a self-study if that self-study meets rigorous QAS standards, and is offered by a QAS Sponsor. As of August 5, 2010, KPMG has been approved as a QAS Sponsor by NASBA, and is now able to offer QAS CPE credit for courses that conform to QAS standards.
  • Not all KPMG self-studies conform to QAS requirements. Please refer to Quality Assurance Service (QAS) CPE Credit for instructions on how to locate QAS-eligible self-studies in Focus.
  • QAS standards apply only to Web-based self-studies. All states, including those listed below, will recognize CPE credit that KPMG awards for instructor-led or KLEARN LIVE! training.

State Boards of Accountancy in the following states will only recognize CPE credit for a self-study if that self-study is a QAS-approved course offered by a QAS Sponsor:

  • Florida
  • Minnesota
  • Tennessee
  • Oregon
  • South Carolina

Because KPMG is now a QAS sponsor, the firm is now able to offer CPE credit for some internally-produced self-studies. In addition, all self-studies produced by certain external vendors are eligible for QAS CPE credit.

Note: QAS sponsorship is for self-study programs only. There is no QAS sponsorship for instructor-led training or virtual classroom/KLEARN LIVE! training.
